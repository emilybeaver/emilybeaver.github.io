In-Person Services

I offer in-person organizing services for San Francisco residents (I serve other Bay Area locations on a case-by-case basis, please inquire for details). Services include:

  • Deciding what to keep vs. discard
  • Purging clutter
  • Organization systems for every room
  • Zero-waste pantries
  • Capsule/seasonal wardrobes
  • Designing effective home offices
  • Beautiful, effective closet organization
  • Responsible disposal of unwanted items
  • Donating and selling unwanted items
  • Move in/out assistance

Virtual Services

If you live outside of San Francisco or simply prefer to take a do-it-yourself approach, I offer virtual organizing services to make your organization project easier, more affordable, and more successful:

  • Virtual coaching: After your free consultation, we'll have a series of check-ins (by video chat or phone) to make sure your project is on track/troubleshoot your issues.
  • Comprehensive organizing e-books: I have several affordable e-books that will give you guidance for your project as well as save you time searching for how to discard/dispose of clutter.

Pricing

I charge by session, a 3-hour block of time. In our consultation, I'll recommend an estimated number of sessions based on your project (the actual time needed to complete your project may vary based on several factors, including how long it takes to make decisions and if you choose to complete work on your own between sessions.)
